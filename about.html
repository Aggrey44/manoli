Introduction

Manoli Youth Ghana (MYG) is a registered Non-Governmental Organization (NGO) in Ghana, committed to addressing youth unemployment and promoting economic independence through skill training and community mobilization. Established in 2008 as a youth club, we transitioned into an NGO in 2019 to expand our impact on a larger platform.

Mission

To empower the youth economically through income generation initiatives such as skill training, apprenticeship, and social development through community mobilization and group support.

Vision

To create economically independent youth who promote national development.

Our Journey

Since our inception, MYG has promoted numerous community initiatives within the Kpone-Katamanso Municipality, supporting individuals in skill development and startup businesses. We currently manage the Manoli Youth Club with over 200 members within the Greater Accra Region, serving as a mentorship group and a catalyst for societal transformation.
