Youth Skill Development

We address unemployment by providing practical training, mentorship, and entrepreneurship skills in two main areas:

Fashion Designing Training

A 24-month certificate program in Fashion Designing.

Mobile Phones Repairs and Computer Hardware Training

A 24-month certificate program in Mobile Phone Repairs and Computer Hardware.

Training Sessions

Morning Session: 7:00 am - 2:00 pm

Afternoon Session: 2:30 pm - 8:00 pm

General Courses

  • Entrepreneurship skill development
  • General life development and motivational lectures

Mentorship

We offer a two-year mentorship program for trainees after completion.

Admissions Requirements

  • Minimum of SSCE or WASSCE Certificate, and a selection interview.
  • BECE Certificate holders must be at least 24 years old, and pass a selection interview.
  • Age range: 18-35 years.

Formal Education Support

We provide financial support and empowerment to school dropouts and vulnerable families to ensure children can access education. We also educate youth on the positive use of the internet and social media through workshops and seminars.

Environmental Protection

We organize community workshops to educate citizens on green environments, good sanitation practices, and healthy lifestyles.

Community Mobilization

Our core objective is youth development and societal behavioral change. We identify societal problems through research and develop timely solutions by mobilizing community resources.
